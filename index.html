About The School

Built for confident, curious children.

Preencemeka Montesorri supports each child academically, socially, and emotionally. Our classrooms are structured for discovery, independence, language growth, numeracy confidence, and respectful relationships.

Teachers guide children through purposeful activities, creative projects, reading routines, outdoor play, and character-building moments that make learning feel natural and memorable.

Learning Pathways

Programs for every early milestone.

Age 2-3

Pre-Nursery

Gentle routines, sensory play, language development, music, movement, and early independence.

Age 3-5

Nursery

Phonics, early writing, counting, practical life skills, art, storytelling, and guided exploration.

Age 5+

Primary

Strong foundations in literacy, mathematics, science, social studies, technology, and leadership.

What children experience daily

  • Respectful classroom culture
  • Reading and communication practice
  • Creative arts and purposeful play
  • Outdoor activity and social confidence
  • Clean routines, safety, and care
